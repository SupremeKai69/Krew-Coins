SSK is a Hawaii-based squad of specially talented individuals in the state of Hawaii. Founded in 2004, the krew is known for its stunningly attractive and highly athletic members.[1]

Started by the Horsemen and surf enthusiasts, SSK Tokens began by manufacturing and marketing specific coins related to digital currency[2]. It has grown to a following that inspires millions and millions and millions of youth today.[3]

Notable products[edit]

SSK produced the most mutant sized sprays in history, nicknamed the face-slapper. It is thee fattest chunk of salt water which produces a recoil equivalent to shooting ten double-barrel shotguns at the same time![4] SSK minted 1,000,000 of these tokens, and no longer produces the coin.[5][6]
